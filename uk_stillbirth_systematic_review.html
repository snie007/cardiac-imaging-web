Provisional synthesis (UK-focused)
  • Strong recurring signal for socioeconomic and ethnic inequalities in stillbirth burden.
  • Cause-specific and area-level analyses support a deprivation gradient.
  • Service-level bundles and quality-improvement pathways show potential benefit, but implementation and context heterogeneity remain important.
  • Next iteration should add full dual-reviewer screening, standardized risk-of-bias assessment, and pooled effect estimates where methodologically coherent.
