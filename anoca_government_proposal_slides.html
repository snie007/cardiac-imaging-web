Proposed Government Programme Targets (3 years)

  • Target 1: Increase access to definitive ANOCA/INOCA diagnostic pathways in women presenting with stable angina.
  • Target 2: Reduce misclassification as non-cardiac through structured referral + endotyping.
  • Target 3: Deploy registry-linked outcomes monitoring (hospitalizations, repeat testing, quality of life).
  • Target 4: Mandate sex-disaggregated reporting and performance dashboards across sites.
